Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I subscribe?

Finding a visa-sponsored job is difficult, and it takes a lot of effort to search on the internet. At UK Visa Jobs, we did all the hard work for you so that you can focus on applying for jobs. In order to do this, we have put resources in place to speak to employers and verify vacancies to confirm they sponsor work visas. While you can use some features of the website for free, to get access to the entire job list, you will need to subscribe. You will also have a lot of features and get access to webinars and events with your subscription

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your membership anytime by visiting your account page. You can find the cancel button when you click the Manage Subscription tab.

Do all jobs sponsor a work visa?

All of our full-time vacancies, including graduate and experienced hire jobs, have been verified and offer sponsorship. Internships and placements are mainly for students who are studying in the UK, though certain consulting companies and investment banks can sponsor a work visa for internship and placement positions. Whether or not to offer a visa sponsorship depends on a company's policy, and it is subject to change without any prior notice. If you see a position that does not offer sponsorship anymore, please let us know.

How long does it take to find a job?

It depends on your circumstances. Usually, students and graduates spend 3-4 months before securing a job offer, and experienced professionals spend 5-6 months on average. This is calculated based on the information provided by UK Visa Jobs members who have secured job offers. The figure provided is an average time frame, meaning it may take anywhere between 4 weeks to several months.

What do live webinars/workshops cover?

We run weekly live webinars to teach our members how to create a CV and prepare for interviews and assessment centres. Submitting an application for a visa-sponsored job is only the beginning. If you are selected by an employer, you will need to undergo multiple interview stages to secure a job offer. If you do not know how to interview, for example, you may lose the opportunity. We want our members to succeed. That's why we run webinars to help them be better prepared for what's coming.

What is Live Personalised Career Advice?

Members who subscribe to the biannual plan will have access to our weekly open calls, where they can talk to our career advisors one-to-one. During these sessions, the member can ask any questions regarding their specific circumstances and difficulties they encounter on their job application journey to get tailored advice. Our members told us that personalised advice helped them stay focused and increased their chance of getting a job offer considerably.

How often do you update the list?

We add and remove jobs frequently. Therefore, you should apply for the job you are interested in as soon as you see it. It is important to remember that some jobs are not as readily available as others. For example, marketing jobs may not be as common as software engineering jobs. It does not mean visa-sponsored marketing jobs do not exist. If you do not see a suitable job, you will need to be patient and check the vacancy list again the next day. We also highlighted hard-to-find jobs, like marketing and HR, in our weekly featured jobs list when they become available.

Where can I find more information?

You can find more information on our About&FAQs page.
