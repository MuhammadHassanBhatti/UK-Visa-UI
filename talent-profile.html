Talent Profile

(This step is optional. If you do not want to register your talent profile, please press Submit to complete your registration.)

What is it?

UK Visa Jobs works with many visa sponsoring organisations to help them source candidates. When a recruiter wants to interview someone, we will shortlist candidates for them based on your talent profile. This will help us find the right candidate for recruiters and help you find the right job.

How does it work?

Once you have submitted your informaiton, if a recruiter is looking for a candidate that matches your talent profile, we will send your details to them. We will ask for your permission before we share any information.

If the employer finds you suitable for the job, we will reach out to you to arrange an interview.

If you are successful in your interview, you will receive a formal job offer, and the recruiter will deal with your paperwork and visa application.

Important:

You should still keep actively applying for jobs on UK Visa Jobs. Submitting a talent profile does not mean the recruiter will want to interview you.

Although we are committed to reviewing all submissions, due to work volume, we prioritise those who have an active subscription with UK Visa Jobs.
